Now, Sofia randomly selects **two rows** from `hundred`, independently
and **with replacement**. What is the probability that Sofia selects at
least one school with a `"human"` mascot? Give your answer as a
**mathematical expression**, which you do not need to simplify.

# BEGIN SOLUTION

**Answer**:

$$1 - \left(\frac{60}{100}\right)^2 = 1 - \left(\frac{3}{5}\right)^2 = 
            1 - \frac{9}{25}
            = \frac{16}{25}
            = 0.64$$
