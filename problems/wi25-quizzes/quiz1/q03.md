One of the clubs in the `clubs` DataFrame is the `"Surf Club"`.
**Without** querying, write an expression that evaluates to the rating
of this club.

# BEGIN SOLUTION

**Answer**: `clubs.set_index("Name").get("Rating").loc["Surf Club"]`

<average>62</average>

the `"Surf Club"`.

# BEGIN SOLUTION
